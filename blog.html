Effective learning method?

13 Dec
  • Post by Anhh Phapp
  • Tagged in Web Design
  • Comments (10)

A notebook to record exercises and to-do tasks is never redundant. It will be a reminder to help you ensure that nothing important is forgotten. Write it down on paper and classify and arrange your work based on the time spent on it. Writing things down on paper can greatly reduce the load on your brain. You can number each job in the order in which it is performed. Or you can also divide tasks according to size and time to solve tasks from few to many, from small to large. And remember, set a deadline for each of the above tasks and follow the plan you set.
